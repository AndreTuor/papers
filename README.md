Microsystem Engineer with a PhD in the field of Semiconductor Physics and Technology from EPFL (École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, Switzerland), I have been active in science and technology all my life. I started this journey when I was very young by building electronic circuits and my own computer based on a Z80, slightly before the very beginning of the mass computer age in the 1980s.

I opened this account to archive some of my work in the field of data-driven signal processing for mechanical systems vibration monitoring, and more to come.
